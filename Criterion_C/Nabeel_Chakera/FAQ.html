FAQ

Account Management

How do I sign up as a mentor or mentee on lemm.ee?

You can sign up as a mentor or mentee by visiting the registration page on lemm.ee and following the prompts to create your profile.

How do I update my personal information on my lemm.ee profile?

To update your personal information, log in to your account, navigate to your profile settings, and make the necessary changes.

Can I view other mentees’ or mentors' profiles on lemm.ee?

Yes, you can view the profiles of other mentees and mentors by searching for them in the community section of lemm.ee.

How do I reset my password on lemm.ee?

If you forget your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset it.

How do I delete my account on lemm.ee?

To delete your account, please contact our support team via the Contact Us page for assistance.

Mentorship Program

What are the requirements to become a cycling mentor on lemm.ee?

To become a cycling mentor, you need to have significant cycling experience and be willing to share your knowledge and skills with mentees.

How can I find a cycling mentor in my area through the lemm.ee platform?

You can search for mentors in your area by using the search function in the mentorship section of the lemm.ee platform.

Is there a fee to join the cycling mentorship program on lemm.ee?

No, joining the cycling mentorship program is free of charge.

What skills or techniques can a mentor help me develop through lemm.ee?

Mentors can help you develop various skills, including cycling techniques, fitness training, and route planning.

Can I switch from being a mentee to a mentor in the program on lemm.ee?

Yes, once you feel confident in your skills and knowledge, you can apply to become a mentor.

What types of cycling events are organized on lemm.ee?
